The Eyrie


An ancient castle and seat of House Arryn, the Eyrie is the smallest compared to the seats of other Great Houses in Westeros, but it’s considered impregnable to attack nonetheless.

The Twins


One of the most formidable strongholds of the Seven Kingdoms, the Twins (or The Crossing) is the seat of House Frey. The Crossing consists of two identical castles and a tower in the middle of their bridge.
—
LORDS: Walder Frey /

The Dothraki Sea


Also known as the Haunted Lands and the Great Desolation, the Dothraki Sea is a landscape of steppes and plains home to the nomadic Dothraki equestrian people, who constantly battle each other and raid surrounding lands.

Village of the Lhazareen


Located west of Lhazar, the Lhazareen camps, who are home to peaceful sheep herders, are frequently raided for slaves by the Dothraki, who refer to them as haesh rakhi or Lamb Men.
